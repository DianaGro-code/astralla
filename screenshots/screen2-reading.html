Paris, France

City reading for Diana G. · Jun 15, 1990

✦ Overview

Paris activates your Venus line — the city asks you to soften, to be seen, to let beauty matter. It won't push your ambitions forward, but it will remind you who you are when no one needs anything from you.

♀ Love & Relationships

Your Venus in the 7th here makes Paris magnetic for romance. Connections feel fated, effortless. You attract people who mirror your ideals back at you — which is both the gift and the danger of this place.

☉ Career & Ambition

Your Sun is in the 12th in Paris — not a power seat, but a contemplative one. Better for creative or private work than public-facing roles. The city rewards depth over speed here.

☽ Inner Life

Your Moon trines the ASC — emotionally, you breathe easier here. Paris supports rest and integration. Old feelings surface, but gently. Good for writing, therapy, or any inner work.
